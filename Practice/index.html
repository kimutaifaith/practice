What is ElevateHer?

ElevateHer is an initiative dedicated to empowering women in tech and STEM by providing mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities. It aims to bridge the gender gap by supporting women at all career stages, fostering skill development, and creating inclusive spaces for growth and innovation. Through workshops, mentorship programs, and advocacy, ElevateHer strives to break barriers and elevate women to leadership positions in the industry.
